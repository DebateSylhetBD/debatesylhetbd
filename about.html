About DebateSylhetBD

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Provide high-quality Bangla debate topics and educational resources to students across Bangladesh, enhancing learning and academic excellence.

Vision

Become the most trusted Bangla educational platform for debates and resources, making learning interactive, engaging, and accessible for everyone.

History

Founded by Rizwan Rahim Chowdhury as a student initiative, DebateSylhetBD focuses on comprehensive debate topics and educational guidance for learners nationwide.

Key Features

Comprehensive Bangla debate topics

Educational guides & resources

Accessible for all students online

Latest educational news

Curated high-quality content

Community & student engagement
