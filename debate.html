The best topics usually revolve around current events and student life. Popular examples include: "Is online education an effective substitute for classroom learning?", "Should mobile phones be banned in schools?", and "The impact of social media on moral values."

Are these topics suitable for college and university debates? ▼

Absolutely. Alongside school-level topics, we cover advanced socio-economic, political, and technological themes such as "The role of AI in future job markets," "Global warming mitigation," and "University research vs. traditional exams."
