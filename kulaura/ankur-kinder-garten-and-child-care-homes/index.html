About Ankur KG School (অঙ্কুর কেজি স্কুল)

Ankur Kinder Garten and Child Care Homes (অংকুর কিন্ডার গার্টেন এন চাইল্ড কেয়ার হোমস) began its profound journey in 1990 as a single-room kindergarten with a visionary goal: to blend values-based education with creative exploration. Today, it stands as a pillar of educational excellence in Kulaura, spanning vibrant playrooms, expansive nature courtyards, and a dedicated child care home that keeps the warmth and comfort of an extended family vibrantly alive.

আমাদের শিক্ষাদান পদ্ধতি প্রতিটি শিশুর নিজস্ব ছন্দকে সম্মান করে। দ্বিভাষিক (Bilingual) শিক্ষার মাধ্যমে আমরা শিক্ষার্থীদের আন্তর্জাতিক মানের জন্য প্রস্তুত করি, পাশাপাশি বছরব্যাপী উৎসবের মাধ্যমে বাংলার সাংস্কৃতিক স্পন্দনকে টিকিয়ে রাখি। অঙ্কুর কেজি স্কুল দীর্ঘ ৩৬ বছর ধরে কুলাউড়ায় শিক্ষার আলো ছড়িয়ে দিচ্ছে।

In our pursuit of excellence, we emphasize an ultra-educational approach. From state-of-the-art pedagogical methods to community-integrated learning, our Ankur kg school campus is a place where academic rigor meets emotional intelligence. Our pedagogy respects every child's rhythm, celebrates bilingual expression, and sustains Bengal's cultural heartbeat through year-round festivities.

Our Journey & Milestones

1990 — The Genesis

The foundation of অংকুর কিন্ডার গার্টেন এন চাইল্ড কেয়ার হোমস was laid. What started as a modest initiative quickly grew due to our uncompromising commitment to child development.

2015 — Community Festivals Flourish

We launched heritage festivals, eco drives, and grandparents' days that turned our Kulaura campus into a celebration ground every season. This holistic approach is why many consider us the premier choice in Moulvibazar.

2024 — Celebrating 35 Years & Beyond

A year of celebration that honours the smiles, songs, and milestones of every অংকুর child and family. A heartfelt look at the joy-filled faces and colourful traditions made our 35th year unforgettable, paving the way for our glorious 36 years complete celebrations.

The Ankur Documentary & Future Vision

The legacy of our institution has been so profound that the upcoming Ankur documentary aims to capture the essence of our 36-year educational journey. This visual archive will showcase how অঙ্কুর কেজি স্কুল transformed the educational landscape in Kulaura. From our humble beginnings to becoming a leading child care home, the Ankur documentary will feature interviews with alumni, teachers, and founders, highlighting the true impact of the Ankur kg school methodology.

Whether you are planning a visit, looking to enrol your little one, or partnering for community events, our organizers are ready to help you. We continuously strive to maintain an ultra-level standard of education, blending modern CSS (Creative Student Solutions) with traditional ethics.
